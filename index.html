✎ EDIT MODE
Tap a pin to edit it. Drag to move. Use Add, then tap the map to drop a new pin.

Edit mode — how it works

  • Move a pin: drag it anywhere on the map.
  • Open a pin: tap it to edit its name, description, time, and icon type.
  • Add a pin: tap ＋ Add marker, then tap the spot on the map.
  • Duplicate: open a pin and tap Duplicate (great for multiple restrooms). Or press Ctrl/⌘+C then Ctrl/⌘+V.
  • Delete: open a pin and tap Delete.
  • Save your changes: tap ⬇ Export, copy the text, and paste it over everything in locations.js. Commit & push. Changes can't auto-save — this site is static.
